Dataset Overview

The AMOS dataset includes two main challenges: AMOS22 and AMOS-MM.

AMOS22 provides 500+ CT & 100+ MRI scans with 15 abdominal organs annotated (for semantic segmentation).

The AMOS-MM dataset includes 2000+ CT scans with comprehensive radiology reports (Findings & Impressions) covering Chest/Abdomen/Pelvis regions (for medical report generation), and 19,562 vision questions (for medical vision question answering).

Evaluation Metrics

Segmentation Metrics
• Dice Score: Measures spatial overlap between predicted and ground truth segmentations
• Normalized Surface Distance (NSD): Evaluates boundary accuracy of segmentation results
Report Generation Metrics
• GreenScore: Comprehensive evaluation of generated medical reports, considering both clinical accuracy and linguistic quality
Vision Question Answering Metrics
• Accuracy: Measures the correctness of model responses to medical visual questions across different clinical scopes
