What is AMOS?

AMOS is a large-scale abdominal segmentation, and captioning dataset. AMOS has several features:

  • ✓ 500+ CT & 100+ MRI scans with 15 abdominal organs annotated
  • ✓ 2000+ CT scans with clinical reports (Findings & Impressions) covering Chest/Abdomen/Pelvis regions
  • ✓ 19,562 vision questions across 6 clinical scopes
